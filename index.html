Unlike the rest, the stars know me best.

Look to the sky to understand why.

My eyes met yours

and I felt it was true.

Even though it's so hard to look at you,

I can't wait until

you're in my view.

It took me by surprise

that someone like you

would show up in my life.

Those round glasses and your thin mustache.

You asked if you can sit,

But you never said a word after it.
